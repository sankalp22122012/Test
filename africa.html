Africa

Africa is second largest and second most populated continents. The population of Africa is about 1,340,598,147.

Covering an area of about 11.7 million square miles, it covers a total of 6% of Earth's total surface area. It is considered as an important Economic market at global level despite of low wealth concentration.

Africa is known to have great variety of terrain, wildlife and climates. It has about 54 states(countries, 8 territories and two de facto independent states with limited or no recognition. Algeria is Africa's largest country by area, and Nigeria is its largest by population.
